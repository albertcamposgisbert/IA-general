For instance, 23∗(2+003)+15∗423*(2+003)+15*423∗(2+003)+15∗4 is a well-formed expression, whereas 13∗∗1513**1513∗∗15 and )(3)(3)(3 are not. For simplicity, in this problem we exclude the use of +++ and −-− as unary operators, such that we also consider +3+3+3 or −5-5−5 as non-well-formed expressions.
